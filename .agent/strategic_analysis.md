### **The Critical Transition: Level 3 → Level 4**

**This is where most fail.**

Going from "agent executes my plans" to "agent makes strategic decisions" requires:

1. **Data foundations** - Agent needs access to reliable metrics
2. **Judgment calibration** - You've corrected enough agent decisions that it learns your values
3. **Community trust** - People are comfortable with AI making decisions
4. **Clear guardrails** - You've defined what the agent can/can't do
5. **Rollback capability** - You can reverse bad decisions without chaos

**My recommendation:**
- Spend Q1-Q2 at Level 1-2 (build foundations)
- Hit Level 3 by end of Q3 (this is the goal for 2026)
- Only attempt Level 4 if Level 3 is working smoothly

**Don't rush to Level 4.** The business value is in proving Levels 1-3 work reliably.
