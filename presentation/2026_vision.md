## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Potential Challenges

**Challenge:** Securing sufficient funding
**Mitigation:** 
- Diversified revenue streams
- Early sponsor outreach (already in progress)
- Scalable event plans (can adjust scope based on funding)
- Grant applications to tech foundations

**Challenge:** Volunteer burnout
**Mitigation:**
- Growth Agent automates repetitive tasks
- Rotate responsibilities among volunteers
- Build larger volunteer base (50+ core volunteers)
- Provide volunteer appreciation and support

**Challenge:** Low hackathon/conference attendance
**Mitigation:**
- Aggressive marketing (6 months out)
- Partner with universities for student participation
- Incentivize early registration
- Hybrid options for remote participation

**Challenge:** Agent development delays
**Mitigation:**
- Phased approach (can work with partial functionality)
- Open source development (community contributions)
- Fallback to manual processes if needed
- Experienced developers leading the project

**Challenge:** Lack of lasting impact from hackathons
**Mitigation:**
- Partner commitments to deploy solutions
- Post-hackathon support programs
- Dedicated project maintenance teams
- Long-term tracking and reporting
